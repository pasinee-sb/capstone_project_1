ReddiSenti, powered by TextBlob, is a sentiment analysis tool for examining Reddit text. It identifies and extracts subjective opinions from Reddit posts about various topics, brands, or products, offering valuable insights into public sentiment.

ReddiSenti Applications

  • Helping beauty companies gauge customer satisfaction.
  • Assisting political campaigns in tracking candidate sentiment.
  • Aiding financial analysts in market trend predictions.
